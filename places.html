Famous Places

Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva, the temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake. Thousands of devotees visit the temple every day.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra located about 70 km from Solapur. It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is located about 40 km from Solapur and is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj. Devotees believe Swami Samarth blesses people and helps them overcome difficulties.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort, also called Bhuikot Fort, is a historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period. It represents the rich historical heritage of Solapur.
